Why we built this

The world needs a fitness standard as trusted as a credit score.

My health insurance premium is based on my age. Not my fitness level. Not my health history. Just my age. Meanwhile, a credit score — built on open, standardized methodology — determines my mortgage rate. We built an entire financial system around a trusted number for money. We’ve never done it for the body.

Golf has a handicap. Bowling has an average. Chess has an Elo rating. Tennis is actively fighting over its standard right now — UTR versus WTN. Fitness, the single greatest indicator of overall health, has nothing.

REALFIT is the institution that closes that gap. Not by telling people how to train — but by creating the common language, the open infrastructure, and the trusted certification framework that makes fitness data mean something for the first time.

The REALFIT Absolute Score is Version 1. The proof of concept. The destination is a world where every fitness standard — for any population, any sport, any occupation — is built on open, trusted, certifiable infrastructure.

Certification

Anyone can calculate a score.
Only REALFIT can certify one.

The methodology is completely open. The scoring tables are published. Any developer can implement it. Transparency is the foundation of trust. But a certified result is different — and that difference is everything for institutional use.

Self-reported estimate

Anyone, anywhere, any time.

Enter your own results at realfitscore.com and see your estimated REALFIT Absolute Score instantly. Useful for self-assessment and goal-setting. Not valid for institutional, competitive, or professional use.

Certified result

Administered. Verified. Credentialed.

A REALFIT Certified Official administers every test in person, according to FITDAT protocol. The certified score carries a verified badge, is valid for 90 days, and is the only result recognised for institutional use. Enquire about Official training →
