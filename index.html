The problem we are solving

Fitness testing exists everywhere.
Standards are inconsistent and often subjective.

The military tests recruits. Fire departments screen candidates. Sports programs run combines. Gyms assess members. The data exists in abundance. But every organization measures differently. Results stay isolated. A result produced in one context means nothing in the next.

This is a language problem. You cannot solve the data problem without a common language. FITDAT® is that language — the open vocabulary that makes every fitness test, result, and standard comparable across any context, population, or purpose.

Our solution approach

The foundation
we are building.

Solving the fitness data problem requires more than an app or a scoring system. It requires a full infrastructure stack — from the language that defines what a test is, to the authority that certifies what a result means, to the community that makes it all real. These are the core capabilities that make up that foundation.

Authority

The trusted standard-setter

A governing body independent of any sport, program, or commercial interest. REALFIT establishes the definitions, procedures, and measuring techniques that make fitness data trustworthy and defensible. Rigorous adherence to standards is the foundation of everything else.

Language

A common language for fitness

FITDAT® — the open vocabulary for tests, protocols, metrics, and standards. Any platform, organization, or institution can share it. Open, governed, and designed to grow. The language has to come first. fitdat.com

Toolkit

Tools for builders & organizations

A replacement for the Excel spreadsheets and PDFs most organizations use today. Score set builder, API access, website templates, private-label options. Build and distribute your own fitness program on the REALFIT foundation.

Library

Fitness tests, protocols, and score sets — searchable by sport, objective, and population. Military standards, professional sports combines, first responder screens, general population benchmarks. Always current. Always open.

Community

A language only becomes a standard when enough people use it. Athletes, coaches, officials, developers, and organizations — the community transforms an open specification into a global standard. The community is not a feature. It is the mission.

Marketplace

Who we serve

Many paths to fitness.
We measure them all.

REALFIT is built for anyone who takes fitness measurement seriously — from the individual who wants a real number to the institution that needs defensible, comparable data at scale.

Individuals & athletes
Anyone who wants to know where they actually stand. Not just a bench max or a mile time — a real, comparable, certified measure of overall physical fitness that travels with you, tracks over time, and holds up against an objective standard. No adjustments for effort. No participation trophies.
Personal trainers & coaches
Give your clients something more meaningful than subjective feedback. REALFIT lets you build branded assessments, establish baselines, track progress with certified data, and give the people you train a number that means something outside your gym.
Military & first responders
The military, law enforcement, and fire service have always tested fitness — but every branch, department, and agency does it differently. REALFIT provides the infrastructure to make those standards consistent, comparable, and institutionally trustworthy. Your existing test is already a score set. We give it a home.
Gyms & fitness programs
CrossFit affiliates, boutique studios, workout programs, and gym chains. Build your own branded diagnostic on the REALFIT foundation — assessments that produce real, comparable results your community can track over time and stand behind beyond your walls.
Corporate wellness & employers
Self-insured communities, corporations, and organizations that recognize fitness as the single best indicator of overall health. REALFIT provides the objective data infrastructure for structured wellness programs, fitness incentives, and population-level fitness tracking — with numbers that actually hold up.
Sports programs & combines
Professional sports combines, elite school programs, and competitive fitness events. REALFIT provides the scoring infrastructure, data analytics, and virtual participation tools so your event produces results that are comparable across time, location, and cohort.
Developers & the broader ecosystem
Fitness app developers, equipment makers, and wearable platforms. Build on FITDAT and connect device data to certified standards. And further out: insurers, financial institutions, and anyone who wants fitness as a recognized, trustworthy variable in health and risk models. The long-term vision is fitness data that finally compounds at scale.
What we have built

Proof that the platform works.

REALFIT has built a series of applications on top of FITDAT to demonstrate what the ecosystem makes possible. Each one is a different expression of the same language.

FITDAT®
The open language of fitness. A growing library of test specifications, protocol definitions, score sets, and population standards — openly published and continuously updated. Developer guidelines and tools allow the community to contribute and expand the library over time. FITDAT is not a snapshot. It is a living, evolving data source. The foundation everything else is built on.
REALFIT Absolute Score™
The first score set published by REALFIT. A general-population fitness benchmark — 13 tests, 0–1,000 scale — built openly on FITDAT with full methodology exposed. Our proposal for a universal fitness standard. One expression of the language, not the language itself.
REALFIT iOS app
The full certified experience. Certified scores, complete history, QR athlete comparison, component analytics, target score planner, and PDF assessment reports.
realfitscore.com
Free self-reported score estimator. All 13 tests, same scoring engine as the app. No account required. Self-reported estimates only — certified results require a REALFIT Official.
ScoreMyFitness.com
Animated, gamified single-test experiences demonstrating what FITDAT-powered consumer applications can look like. Bench press, med ball throw, 40 yard dash.

Coming next: RF Pro (individual access to the full library), RF Official (certification training and credentialing), occupational standards for fire, military, and law enforcement.

What we believe

Fitness deserves
real standards.

We believe the world is better when people are more fit. Not a particular kind of fit — not one body type, one sport, or one program. Genuinely, measurably fit, at every level and in every context.

We believe fitness measurement should be objective and rigorous. Not approximate. Not adjusted for comfort. Not designed to make everyone feel good about where they are. A number that means something — because it was earned against real standards, with consistent methodology, administered the same way for everyone.

We believe in celebrating what humans are capable of at the top end of the range — the strength of the world’s strongest athletes, the endurance of elite distance runners, the agility of elite field performers — and in providing a framework that lets every person measure themselves honestly against those markers, wherever they are on the spectrum.

We believe the shift that matters is from “minimum acceptable performance” to “how high can you raise the bar.” That shift requires data. Real data. Consistent, comparable, institutionally trustworthy data. That is what we are building.

REALFIT is independent of any training philosophy, sport, or commercial interest. We do not tell you how to train. We do not endorse programs. We do not issue attaboys. We measure. We certify. We publish the standards. What you do with it is up to you.
