Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

REAL FIT INC. (“REALFIT,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our website (www.realfit.com) and our mobile applications, including the REALFIT SCORE app (collectively, the “Services”).

By using our Services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1.Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Email address (used for account creation and authentication)

Fitness and Performance Data

  • User-entered fitness data (e.g., time, repetitions, weight, distance)
  • Calculated fitness scores and related analytics

Technical Information

  • Device type, operating system, and app usage data
  • IP address and general location (non-precise)

You may choose not to provide certain information, but some features of the Services may not function properly without it.

2.How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate REALFIT Services

  • Send transactional communications (such as login links or important account notifications)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information for marketing purposes.

3.Account Authentication

REALFIT uses passwordless email authentication (“magic link” login). When you enter your email address, we send a secure login link to authenticate your account.

4.Account Deletion

Users may delete their account at any time directly within the REALFIT mobile application.

Upon deletion:

  • Your account and associated data are permanently removed from our systems
  • Certain data may be retained where required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as fraud prevention or security)

5.Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to operate our Services, including:

  • Supabase — authentication and data storage

These providers process data only as necessary to provide their services and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

6.Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our Services and fulfill legal obligations.

7.Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8.Children’s Privacy

REALFIT Services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9.Your Rights and Choices

You may:

  • Delete your account at any time
  • Contact us with questions or requests regarding your data
